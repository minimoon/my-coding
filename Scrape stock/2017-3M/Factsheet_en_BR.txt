The Company is an integrated producer of premium duck meat products with 5 business lines including 1) feed, 2) parent stock farming, 3) hatchery, 4) commercial duck farming, and 5) slaughterhouse and food processing for duck meat products, with end products as raw duck meat, cooked duck meat including byproducts.
