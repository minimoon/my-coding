<td class="factsheet">JASIF has invested in the rights of 980,000 core kilometers fiber optic network for broadband internet of Triple T Broadband pcl (TTTBB). The 800,000 core kilometers will be transferred to JASIF immediately after the IPO and the additional 180,000 core kilometers will be transferred to JASIF within the next two years. JASIF will manage these assets by having TTTBB lease 80 percent of the fiber optic network via 11-year main lease agreement (ending February 22, 2026). The remaining 20 percent will be subject to the three-year lease income insurance according to the rental assurance agreement, on the condition that JASIF can renew the contract every three years until the main lease agreement expires.</td>
